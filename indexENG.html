    ABOUT US

    Maratona - Founded in 1966, it combines the excellence of the contemporary with its historical and traditional values, being one of the best places in the west of Portugal for your daily meal or snack. Located in the city center of Caldas da Rainha, at Praça 25 de Abril. The restaurant is considered by critics and customers as one of the best in the Center area, having collected the best gastronomy awards of the region several times. The Café + Esplanade is comfortable and ideal for groups or just for a simple coffee, serving all-day tasty light meals with a wide choice of cafeteria and sweets. Local and traditional, vegan and vegetarian food options always available.

    Chef Ricardo

    Chef Ricardo, graduated in Fine Arts in Caldas da Rainha, comes from Porto. He collaborated in different artistic projects, was (or is) a musician and always kept close contact with the kitchen. In the meantime, he was forced to prepare his meals. It has been experimenting and testing, enriching its knowledge in this area. Worked at one of the first Thai restaurants in Portugal. Here you have created the foundations that nowadays influence your kitchen. Obligation, willingness, curiosity and much research were essential decision makers in this cohesive union. Arrived at the Marathon, being the party of the qualities that are inherent in the present passion.
